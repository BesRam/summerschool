Data Example Questions

Example Questions (Data Insights)

  • Provide a summary statistic of all numerical data such as age and year!
  • List the top 5 most frequent shark species involved in attacks!
  • Provide a pivot table with the number of attacks by type and country!
  • What are the top 5 activities people were doing during shark attacks?

Example Questions (Graphics)

  • Show a histogram of victim ages!
  • Create a horizontal bar chart showing top 10 countries by number of attacks!
  • Plot the distribution of shark attacks by type (Unprovoked, Provoked, etc.) in a pie chart!
  • Show a horizontal bar chart of the number of attacks by the top 5 most activities!

Example Questions (Machine-Learning)

  • Use k-means clustering based on the variables age and year (NAs removed) to find groups of shark attack victims.
  • Create a random forest model to predict the type of attack (target) based on country, activity, age, and sex.
  • Fit a logistic regression model to classify the type of attack (target) by age, and sex.
  • Fit a model to predict the two species 'Tiger shark' or 'Bull shark' based on country, activity, age, and sex.
